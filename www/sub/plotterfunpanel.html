Plotterfun

Plotterfun converts your images into SVGs using various rendering styles.

Select your image, when it has loaded, select the renderer (algorithm) and choose the associated settings for that renderer, then click 'Use image' to see a preview of the SVG. When you are happy with the result, click the 'Download SVG' button to save it, then you can convert it to gcode using the built in jscut or an external program.
